What does this calculator do?

Buying a home is one of the largest financial decisions a household makes. This tool compares the financial consequences of buying and renting — objectively, without lifestyle preferences.

Using regional purchase and rental prices from Austrian federal states and districts, it estimates how your wealth develops over the next 30 years.

🗺️
How to use it

📍 Select the Federal States or Districts layer and click a region on the map.

🏠 Review or adjust your household details. Property prices and rent are automatically filled from regional data.

📈 Run the calculation and compare renting vs. buying over time. Find your personal break-even point.
