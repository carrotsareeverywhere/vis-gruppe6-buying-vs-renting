Interactive Map of Austria & Financial Dashboard

Interactive comparison of the long term net-worth difference when purchasing or renting a new Home, in Austria

Many young adults and people who open a new chapter in live, want to move to a new place.

With our interactive Map, which shows a detailed avg. price either per federal state or per district, for renting or buying a Home, we want to enable the user to easily compare prices and get a feeling for the possible long term differences in wealth accumulation. We are therefor putting aside the personal incentives to choose either buying or renting a new home and will focus on the financial aspect.

The red/yellow lines of the Break-Even Chart represent 2 households wealth accumulation based on regional average additional housing costs. Both have no outstanding loans (House = payed off)

How to use: Select an asset layer (States or Districts) and click any target region on the map to extract local real estate trends.

The input section is pre-loaded with default values. To get a more accurate prediction. please fill out your personal info

The price and rent are auto-calculated based on the property size and the avg. rent/buy prices from your selected state/district.

The Loan is also auto-calculated based on your Houshold Equity Capital and the price of the property you want to purchase. You can however change it to a different amount. Please note that for the calculation we will take a loan amount less then the calculated laon amount as "you have additional funds" to purchase the Home.
